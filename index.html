Jeannine Coons

UX/UI Designer and Front-End Developer

Hey there, I'm Jeannine! I'm a student at Kenzie Academy, enrolled in the UIE-4 Cohort. I'm a visual artist, mom and UX Engineer. My experience is varied from customer service and retail to owning my own business and designing websites for local businesses using Wordpress and Wix. The latter left me with a desire to learn more about building websites and developing apps that could help people connect.

I've always felt like I could learn anything worth doing and that's left me with a lot of hobbies. Painting, sculpting, writing, fiber arts and an extensive cooking repertoire means I never run short of things to do, just time to do them in. Being a mom puts even more pressure on my time, but I'd never have it any other way. My two daughters are a driving force for me, it is so important for me to show them that they can do anything that speaks to them. I live to be inspired and to create designs with passion and expertise. All of this is translated into my designs and the work that I do, so that I can create the very best I'm capable of.
