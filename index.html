Hey there, I'm Jeannine! I'm a student at Kenzie Academy, enrolled in the UIE-4 Cohort. I'm a visual artist, mom and UX Engineer. I've always felt like I could learn anything and that's left me with a lot of hobbies. Painting, sculpting, writing, fiber arts and an extensive cooking repertoire means I never run short of things to do, just time to do them in. Being a mom has made me a time-management wizard and I'd never have it any other way. My two daughters are a driving force for me, it is so important for me to show them that they can do anything that speaks to them. I live to be inspired and to create designs with passion and expertise. All of this is translated into my designs and the work that I do, so that I can create the very best I'm capable of.
